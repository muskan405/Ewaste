Consumer Electronic Waste

Consumers can recycle computers, peripherals and televisions at an EPSA collection point and be assured devices will be recycled safely, efficiently and to the highest environmental standards. Electronic Product Stewardship Australasia (EPSA) is an approved Co-Regulatory Arrangement under the Australian Government Product Stewardship Act and is wholly owned by Sims E-Recycling Pty Ltd, trading as Sims Recycling Solutions (SRS).
